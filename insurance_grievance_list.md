## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **IRDAI Grievance Portal (Bima Bharosa):** All insurers accept complaints through the unified IRDAI portal at https://bimabharosa.irdai.gov.in/

2. **IRDAI Grievance Call Centre (IGCC):**
   - Toll-free Number: 155255 or 1800-425-4732
   - Email: complaints@irdai.gov.in
   - Available for escalation if insurer response is unsatisfactory

3. **Insurance Ombudsman:** If grievance is unresolved within 15 days, approach the Insurance Ombudsman at https://www.cioins.co.in/

4. **Contact Accuracy:** All contact details are sourced from official company websites and IRDAI records. Contact numbers and email addresses are subject to change - verify before reaching out.

5. **Response Timeline:** Per IRDAI regulations, insurers must acknowledge complaints within 3 working days and resolve within 14-15 days.

6. **Senior Citizen Support:** Many insurers offer dedicated senior citizen support lines with extended helpline hours.
