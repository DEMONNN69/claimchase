Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026 | Version 2.0

1. Who We Are

AmicusClaims ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered insurance grievance platform that helps users draft, send, and track case-related communications with insurance providers. Our platform is accessible at https://www.amicusclaims.ai.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: amicusclaims.ai@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

a) Account Information

  • Full name and email address
  • Authentication credentials (managed securely)
  • Profile settings and preferences

b) Case and Document Information

  • Insurance case details you provide
  • Documents and files you upload for grievance processing
  • Communication history related to your cases

c) Gmail Data (only if you connect your Gmail account)

  • gmail.send scope: Used solely to send case-related emails on your behalf
  • gmail.metadata scope: Used to read message metadata (labels, headers, sender, timestamps, thread IDs) for reply tracking purposes only
  • We do NOT read, store, or process the body content of your emails beyond what is required to deliver the service

d) Usage Information

  • Pages visited and features used within the platform
  • Device type, browser type, and IP address
  • Session timestamps and activity logs for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate, draft, and send insurance grievance communications on your behalf
  • Track the status of your case communications and replies
  • Improve and personalize your experience on the platform
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access or misuse
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do NOT use your Google user data for:

  • Advertising or remarketing purposes
  • User profiling or behavioral tracking
  • Sale or transfer to any third party for commercial gain
  • Training our AI models without your explicit consent

4. AI Services We Use

AmicusClaims uses the following third-party AI services to power its features:

a) OpenAI (openai.com)

  • Used for: AI-assisted grievance letter drafting, content generation, and communication suggestions
  • Data shared: Relevant case text and prompts you provide within the platform, processed via OpenAI's API
  • OpenAI's Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

b) Google Gemini (deepmind.google/technologies/gemini)

  • Used for: Document analysis, case summarization, and AI-powered content enhancement
  • Data shared: Relevant case documents and inputs you provide, processed via Google's Gemini API
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Important: We only share the minimum data necessary with these AI providers to deliver the requested features. We do not share personal identifiers such as your full name, email address, or financial information with AI services unless you explicitly include them in your content inputs.

5. Google API Data — Limited Use Disclosure

AmicusClaims's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request Gmail permissions that are necessary to deliver the core service features you have requested
  • We do not use Gmail data to develop, improve, or train generalized AI or machine learning models
  • We do not allow humans to read your Gmail data unless you have given us explicit permission, it is necessary for security purposes, or we are required to do so by law
  • We do not share Gmail data with third parties except as necessary to provide and improve user-facing features, as disclosed in this policy

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

a) Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery, AI processing). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may only use it to provide services to us.

b) AI Service Providers

As described in Section 4, we share limited case-related content with OpenAI and Google Gemini to deliver AI-powered features.

c) Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of AmicusClaims, our users, or others.

d) Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services.

  • Account data is retained until you request deletion or close your account
  • Case files and communications are retained for up to 2 years after your last active session, unless you request earlier deletion
  • Gmail metadata is not stored beyond the active session required for reply tracking
  • Anonymized usage logs may be retained for up to 3 years for security and analytics purposes

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at amicusclaims.ai@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to legal obligations requiring us to retain certain records.

8. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS (HTTPS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Strict access controls limiting who can access your data internally
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your account.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at amicusclaims.ai@gmail.com.

Gmail Access: You may disconnect your Gmail account at any time through your AmicusClaims account settings or directly via your Google Account permissions at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us at amicusclaims.ai@gmail.com.
