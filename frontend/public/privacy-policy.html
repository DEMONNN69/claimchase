Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026 | Version 1.0

1. Who We Are

AmicusClaims is an insurance grievance platform that helps users draft, send, and track case-related communications.

2. Google Data Access and Use

After your consent, we use Gmail API scopes only for service functionality:

  • gmail.send: Send case-related emails on your behalf from your connected Gmail account.

We do not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

AmicusClaims' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, authentication details).
  • Case details and documents uploaded by you.

4. How We Use Data

  • Generate and send grievance communications.
  • Track case communication status and replies.
  • Provide platform support and security.

5. Storage, Sharing, and Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit and access controls. We do not sell personal data. We share data only with processors needed to provide the service or as legally required.

6. User Controls

You may disconnect Gmail access at any time via your account settings or through Google account permissions. You may request account/data deletion subject to legal obligations.
